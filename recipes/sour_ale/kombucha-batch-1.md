# Kombucha (Green Tea)
## Batch Number 1
### Started 03-Jan-2013 8:30 EST

### Recipe (Same as batch 0, except added 1/2 cup more sugar)

- 20 green tea bags
- 1.5 cups of sugar
- 1 gallon of water
- 1 symbiotic culture of bacteria and yeast (SCOBY)

### Process
Add sugar to water.

Steep tea at 185F for 2-3 minutes.

Cool sweet tea to 80F.

Add SCOBY.
