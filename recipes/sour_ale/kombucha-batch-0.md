# Kombucha
## Batch Number 0

### Recipe

- 20 green tea bags
- 1 cup of sugar
- 1 gallon of water
- 1 symbiotic culture of bacteria and yeast (SCOBY)

### Process
Add sugar to water.

Steep tea at 185F for 2-3 minutes.

Cool sweet tea to 80F.

Add SCOBY.
